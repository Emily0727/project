Billionaire Tycoon Challenge

Game Objective

Grow your net worth as much as possible through strategic spending and investments within 10 minutes.

Starting Conditions

  • $100 billion in starting cash
  • 10-minute time limit
  • All purchases are final (no refunds)

Scoring System

Your final score is calculated as:

Total Net Worth = Cash + Investment Portfolio Value + (Luxury Assets Value × 0.5)

Highest net worth wins!

Investment Strategy

  • Investments fluctuate randomly based on their volatility
  • High-risk investments (like Bitcoin) can yield big gains or losses
  • Low-risk investments (like Gold) grow slowly but steadily

Special Bonuses

  • Bargain Hunter: Buying sale items gives 5% bonus
  • Trendsetter: First purchase in any category gives 2% bonus
  • Diversification: Own items in 5+ categories for 5% bonus

Penalties

  • Liquidity Crisis: Lose 2% net worth per minute if cash < $1B
  • Overconcentration: Any investment >30% of portfolio increases volatility

Tips for Success

  • Balance cash, investments, and luxury assets
  • Early game: Focus on investments to grow capital
  • Mid game: Diversify across categories
  • Late game: Buy high-value luxury items
